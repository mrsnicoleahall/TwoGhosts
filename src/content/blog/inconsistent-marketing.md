Marketing inconsistency has nothing to do with being busy. Busy people still manage to eat, sleep, and breathe. They do what they prioritize.

# **You Do Not Have a Marketing Problem, You Have a Priorities Problem**

If everything is important, nothing is.

# **Inconsistency Happens Because No One Owns Growth**

If ten people “sort of” own it, no one does. Growth needs a single owner, not a committee.

# **Your Message Changes Every Month**

If you keep reinventing your positioning every time you get bored, your audience never learns who you are.

# **Consistency Requires Structure**

* One owner  
* One message  
* One plan  
* One weekly rhythm  

Consistency is not magic. It is management.
